NFL Blitz 2003

NFL Blitz 2003 is a video game published by Midway Sports for Game Boy Advance, GameCube, PlayStation 2 and Xbox in 2002.





== Reception ==



The game received "average" reviews on all platforms except the Game Boy Advance version, which received "unfavorable" reviews, according to the review aggregation website Metacritic.

It was a runner-up for GameSpot's annual "Best Game No One Played on GameCube" award, which went to Sega Soccer Slam.
