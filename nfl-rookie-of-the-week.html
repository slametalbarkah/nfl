NFL Rookie of the Week

NFL Rookie of the Week, sponsored by Pepsi, is an award given weekly to the best performing rookie players in the National Football League (NFL). Winners are chosen among five finalists every week during the season by an online vote on NFL.com. Total votes by the end of the season are used to determine the five finalists for the Pepsi NFL Rookie of the Year award. The award is sometimes named after other varieties of Pepsi, such as Diet Pepsi and Pepsi Zero Sugar. It has been awarded since 2002. As of the 2024 NFL season, Jayden Daniels holds the record for most awards, earning the award 11 times during his rookie season, passing Ben Roethlisberger.
