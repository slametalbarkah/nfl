Maxx Crosby

Maxx Robert Crosby (born August 22, 1997) is an American professional football defensive end for the Las Vegas Raiders of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football for the Eastern Michigan Eagles and was selected by the Oakland Raiders in the fourth round of the 2019 NFL draft.





== Early life ==

Maxx Robert Crosby was born in Lapeer, Michigan, and moved to Colleyville, Texas during adolescence. The spelling of Crosby's first name comes from how large (11 pounds, 9 ounces) he was at birth. He attended Colleyville Heritage High School in Colleyville, Texas.

Crosby's mother, Vera Crosby, is of Serbian and Albanian origin. Asked about his Serbian roots on his Instagram account, Crosby replied with najbolji smo, najjaci, Serbian for "we are the best, [we are] the strongest". He added an eagle emoticon, the Eagles being a nickname both for the Serbian national sports teams, and for their supporter groups.





== College career ==

Crosby played college football at Eastern Michigan from 2015 to 2018. Eastern Michigan was the only college football program to give Crosby an offer.
