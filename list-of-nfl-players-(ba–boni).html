List of NFL players (Ba–Boni)

This is a list of players who have appeared in at least one regular season or postseason game in the National Football League (NFL), American Football League (AFL), or All-America Football Conference (AAFC) and have a last name that falls between "Ba" and "Boni". For the rest of the B's, see list of NFL players (Bonn–By). This list is accurate through the end of the 2024 NFL season.
